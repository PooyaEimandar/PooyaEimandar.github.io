Pooya's Timeline

Pooya Eimandar, was born on 7th January 1986, graduated in computer science and hardware engineering degree from Shomal university and has been programming mainly in DirectX and OpenGL since 2002. His main research interests are the GPU-programming, image processing, parallel computing and game developing. He began his career working on various 3D simulation applications and since 2010, he has been leading a game engine team in Bazipardaz company’s latest titles. In August 2013 his first book "DirectX 11.1 Game Programming" published by PackT. Currently he is working for AlAlam news network TV. for impelmenting the new generation of Playout & CG editor based on MOS protocol.
